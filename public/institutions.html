🏫 For education institutions

Universities, TAFEs and colleges: register to manage your listing and receive genuinely interested, questionnaire-qualified students. Listing is free — we don’t sell placement and we don’t take commissions on students.

We verify CRICOS registration before listing course information. We never charge students for introductions and never rank providers for money.

How listings work

  1. You register interest with your CRICOS code.
  2. We verify registration on the official register.
  3. Your listing shows factual info: sector, locations, official links.
  4. Students reach you directly — no middle-agent commissions.

Our promise to students is honest, unbiased information; our promise to institutions is qualified interest, not inflated numbers. Those promises only work together.

Transparency: listings show public, factual information and official links only. AusWise receives no commissions from institutions and does not rank providers for payment. Confirm CRICOS registration and course details on the official register.
